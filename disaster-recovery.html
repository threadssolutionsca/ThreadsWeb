Disaster Recovery.

Why Disaster Recovery?

Business continuity and disaster recovery (BCDR) is the process of evaluating an organization's ability to recover and resume normal operations after a disaster or significant system failure. It involves developing comprehensive plans, establishing data backup and recovery solutions, maintaining redundant IT infrastructure, and creating alternate work environments. Regular testing and training ensure preparedness, while minimizing the impact of disruptions and maintaining business resilience. By prioritizing BCDR, organizations can protect assets, mitigate financial and reputational losses, and ensure the continuity of operations in the face of unforeseen events.
